Project Overview

After watching tons of videos online, reading blogs and speaking with people owning an Mazda MX-5 I finally came in the position to become owner of a Mazda MX-5 myself. I have always liked the community around the car and looked forward to add modifications to this car to experiment myself how an already good driving car can become a car which hits you every time you start it, every time you brake hard into a corner, steer into a corner at speed and just be amazed what feedback this car gives you.

I did not had much experience wrenching on cars, but my dad always did the maintenance stuff himself and the whole internet is full of passionate people which wrench on theire MX-5, so with that in mind I started fixing the car and modifiing it to my likings
